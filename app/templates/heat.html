Heat Map

The first step in altering a track represented by a scatter plot is to locate its <plot> block in specific.conf.

You should find the line type = heatmap in that block.

In this track, regions of the genome are assigned a value (e.g. density of genes) and are shown as wedges whose color is a function of the value.

- M. Krzywinski

It is hard to demonstrate the outcomes of some parameters on heatmap, specially color-related ones: for a same color-scheme, heat maps from different data sets will have completely different pattern. It is best to play around and test different outcomes to obtain the best and clearest results.

An easy way to modify your heat map is to give it a specific color scheme. To do so, add the line color = underneath type = heatmap.
