Colors

You can practically use any color that you want! You can even define your own colros with RGB or HSV definitions in the <color> block. If you are not familiar with the latter, it is suggested that you use the pre-defined ones.

These are the eight main colors used by Circos. You can also play with their tone for more variability (except for black and white). To do so, add one of the tone prefixes to the color name.

vvd, vd, d, l, vl, vvl

Where v stands for very, d for dark and l for light.
